Crystal Collector

A game of some measure of strategy.

  1. How to Play

  2. At the start of the game, you will be given a random target number.
  3. There are four crystals, clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to your target number. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the target number.
  5. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  6. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
